Welcome to HopeBot Help

HopeBot is your personal school support assistant at Mengo Senior School. It helps students with:

  • Chat: Ask questions or share your thoughts in a safe environment.
  • Journal: Write and save your personal notes securely.
  • Report: Send anonymous reports or messages to the school counselor.
  • Breathing: Use guided exercises to reduce stress and relax.
  • Mind Games: Play interactive games to improve focus and memory.

How to Use HopeBot

  • Navigate through the menu at the top to access different features.
  • Click the buttons in each section to start or interact with games and exercises.
  • Your journal and reports are saved locally on your device for privacy.
  • Follow on-screen instructions for breathing exercises and games for the best experience.
