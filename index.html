Welcome to HopeBot

HopeBot is a school support assistant designed for School students. It helps you manage stress, emotions, and focus while providing safe tools like chat, journaling, breathing exercises, and fun mind games. Everything here is offline, private, and student-friendly perfect for learning and mental wellbeing.

Chat

Talk with HopeBot about your feelings, studies, or stress safely.

Journal

Write and save your personal thoughts securely and privately.

Anonymous Report

Submit school concerns or report issues safely and anonymously.

Mind Games & Breathing

Relax your mind, improve focus, and manage stress with fun exercises.

About This Project

This project is part of a school initiative to support student mental health and academic focus. HopeBot demonstrates a fully functional offline chatbot system with safe student tools, designed to be explainable, exam-ready, and professional. All interactions are private and focused on improving wellbeing.
